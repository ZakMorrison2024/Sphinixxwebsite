Sphinixx Game Development Roadmap

Phase 1: Conceptualization and Prototyping

  • Duration: 3 months
  • Objective: Lay the foundation for the project, define game concepts, and create initial prototypes to validate ideas.
    • Conduct market research and identify target audience.
    • Define game concepts and storyline for each project:
      • Secret Communal Platform
      • PsYcHoSiS
      • Black and White? (name to be changed)
      • Fear
    • Develop initial game prototypes to test mechanics and gameplay elements.

Phase 2: Development and Production

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Objective: Develop full-scale versions of each game, focusing on gameplay, graphics, and user experience.
    • Begin development of each game concurrently, allocating resources based on project complexity and priorities.
    • Iteratively build and test game mechanics, levels, and features.
    • Implement high-quality graphics, sound effects, and music to enhance immersion.
    • Conduct user testing and gather feedback for iterative improvements.
    • Ensure compatibility across different platforms and devices.

Phase 3: Beta Testing and Optimization

  • Duration: 3 months
  • Objective: Refine gameplay, address bugs, and optimize performance based on user feedback.
    • Launch beta versions of each game to a select group of testers.
    • Gather feedback and analyze user data to identify areas for improvement.
    • Address bugs, glitches, and performance issues promptly.
    • Fine-tune gameplay mechanics, difficulty levels, and user interface based on user input.
    • Conduct extensive playtesting to ensure a polished and enjoyable gaming experience.

Phase 4: Marketing and Launch

  • Duration: Ongoing
  • Objective: Generate buzz and excitement around the upcoming game releases, build anticipation, and maximize visibility.
    • Develop marketing materials, including trailers, screenshots, and promotional artwork.
    • Launch teaser campaigns and engage with the gaming community through social media, forums, and events.
    • Collaborate with influencers and gaming media to generate coverage and reviews.
    • Plan and execute a coordinated launch strategy for each game, including release dates and distribution platforms.
    • Monitor user engagement, reviews, and sales metrics post-launch, and adjust marketing strategies as needed.

Phase 5: Post-Launch Support and Expansion

  • Duration: Ongoing
  • Objective: Provide ongoing support, updates, and expansions to maintain player engagement and drive revenue growth.
    • Monitor player feedback and address issues through regular updates and patches.
    • Plan and develop post-launch content updates, expansions, and DLCs to extend the lifespan of each game.
    • Engage with the community through forums, social media, and events to foster a loyal fanbase.
    • Explore opportunities for cross-promotion, partnerships, and licensing deals to expand the reach of the Sphinixx brand.
