Rewards for Patrons

  1. Custom Character Creation:

    Offer patrons the opportunity to collaborate with you on the creation of a custom game character. They can provide input on the character's appearance, personality, backstory, and abilities.

  2. In-Game Cameo Appearances:

    Reward higher-tier patrons with cameo appearances of themselves or their designed characters in your upcoming games. This could be as non-playable characters (NPCs), side quest givers, or even playable characters, depending on the scope of your project.

  3. Digital Artwork:

    Commission custom digital artwork of the patron's designed character. This could include concept art, character portraits, or full-body illustrations, created by you or a professional artist.

  4. Exclusive Skins or Costumes:

    Offer patrons exclusive in-game skins or costumes based on their character designs. These can be cosmetic items that players can equip to customize the appearance of their in-game characters, adding value to your game and incentivizing patronage.

  5. Character Naming Rights:

    Allow patrons to name in-game characters or locations after themselves or their designed characters. This adds a personal touch to your game world and makes patrons feel directly involved in the creative process.

  6. Behind-the-Scenes Access:

    Provide patrons with behind-the-scenes access to the development process of the game featuring their custom characters. This could include concept art, design discussions, development updates, and beta testing opportunities.
