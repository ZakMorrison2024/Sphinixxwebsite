Cyber_Unknown

An immersive top-down HTML5 game where your optical implant malfunctions, plunging you into darkness. With your threat detection system triggered, survival is your only objective.

Navigate through the unseen dangers of a neon-lit dystopia, relying solely on your instincts to evade threats lurking in the shadows. Engage in strategic battles against unknown adversaries, using every tool at your disposal to stay alive.

In this minimalist game experience, every decision matters as you fight your way through the darkness, determined to emerge victorious against the odds. Can you survive the unknown and reclaim control in Cyber_Unknown?

Controls: LEFT, RIGHT, UP, DOWN - Move | W, A, S, D - Move | Mouse - Aim | MB_Left Click - Shoot/Direction
